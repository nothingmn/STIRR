# Project Ideas

Future projects not yet started — the backlog of possibilities awaiting activation.

## What belongs here

- Project concept notes and pitch documents
- Feasibility sketches and initial scope outlines
- Priority rankings and backlog lists
- "Someday/maybe" items with enough substance to preserve
- Dependencies and prerequisites for future projects

## What does NOT belong here

- Active projects (move to [[../Prototyping/PROTOCOL|Prototyping Protocol]] or a dedicated project space once started)
- Half-formed ideas without project shape (those go to [[../../1. Study/Thought Streams/PROTOCOL|Thought Streams Protocol]])
- Quick experiments already underway (those belong in [[../Prototyping/PROTOCOL|Prototyping Protocol]])
- Completed project records (those go to [[../../7. Archive/Completed Projects/PROTOCOL|Completed Projects Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Bridge mode.** Both human and LLM collaborate on idea development here. The LLM may elaborate concept notes, generate outlines, and assess feasibility — always marking its contributions as LLM-generated.
2. **Link dependencies.** If one idea depends on another being completed first, link them. The LLM should surface dependency chains during review.
