# Prototyping

Quick experiments and proof-of-concept work — the Workshop's active build space.

## What belongs here

- Proof-of-concept logs and notes
- Technical spike results
- Build instructions and environment setup records
- Experiment outcomes and observations
- Active project notebooks for projects still in exploratory phase

## What does NOT belong here

- Project ideas not yet started (those stay in [[../Project Ideas/PROTOCOL|Project Ideas Protocol]])
- Completed projects (those move to [[../../7. Archive/Completed Projects/PROTOCOL|Completed Projects Protocol]])
- Research notes (those belong in [[../../1. Study/Research/PROTOCOL|Research Protocol]])
- Stable reference material (those belong in [[../../2. Library/General Reference/PROTOCOL|General Reference Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Bridge mode.** Human and LLM build together here. The LLM may generate code, draft configurations, and document setup steps — all subject to human testing and validation.
2. **Disposable by design.** Prototypes are meant to be transient. When a prototype graduates to a real project, extract the learnings and archive or discard the prototype artifacts.
