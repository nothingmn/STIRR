# Historical

Closed chapters, former employers, and past contexts — the backdrop of personal and professional history.

## What belongs here

- Former employer records and role descriptions
- Past organizational memberships and affiliations
- Closed life chapters (relocations, career transitions)
- Historical timelines and biographical notes
- Records of past contexts that may inform future decisions

## What does NOT belong here

- Completed projects (those go to [[../Completed Projects/PROTOCOL|Completed Projects Protocol]])
- Web clippings and saved articles (those go to [[../Clippings/PROTOCOL|Clippings Protocol]])
- Content with no remaining reference value (those go to [[../Obsolete/PROTOCOL|Obsolete Protocol]])
- Active professional relationships (those go to [[../../5. Quarters/People/PROTOCOL|People Protocol]] or [[../../4. Gallery/Opportunities/PROTOCOL|Opportunities Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Preserve, don't revise.** Historical notes are a record of what happened, not a place to revise the narrative. Add corrections as addenda, not overwrites.
2. **Index for retrieval.** The LLM indexes historical records for background checks, resume building, and context retrieval. Ensure naming and tagging support these use cases.
