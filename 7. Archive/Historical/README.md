# Historical

Closed chapters, former employers, and past contexts — the backdrop of personal and professional history. This area holds records of closed life chapters: former employers, past roles, expired memberships, and contextual history that may be needed for reference but no longer drives current action. Historical records provide context, not direction.

## What belongs here

- Former employer records and role descriptions
- Past organizational memberships and affiliations
- Closed life chapters (relocations, career transitions)
- Historical timelines and biographical notes
- Records of past contexts that may inform future decisions

## What does NOT belong here

- Completed projects → [[../Completed Projects/README.md|Completed Projects]]
- Web clippings and saved articles → [[../Clippings/README.md|Clippings]]
- Content with no remaining reference value → [[../Obsolete/README.md|Obsolete]]
- Active professional relationships → [[../../5. Quarters/People/README.md|People]] or [[../../4. Gallery/Opportunities/README.md|Opportunities]]

## Tips

- **Closed by definition.** Only material from definitively closed chapters belongs here. If a past employer might be rejoined or a past affiliation might resume, it is not yet historical.
- **Date-bounded context.** Every historical note should specify the time period it covers. "Employment at X, 2018-2021" is explicit; "Time at X" is ambiguous.
- **Preserve, don't revise.** Historical notes are a record of what happened, not a place to revise the narrative. Add corrections as addenda, not overwrites.
- **Low maintenance.** Historical notes should require no ongoing maintenance. If a note needs regular updates, the chapter is not closed.
