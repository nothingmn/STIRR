# Opportunities

Applications, proposals, and leads — the outward-facing pursuits that may become real work. This area tracks professional and creative opportunities: job applications, grant proposals, collaboration leads, pitches, and anything that represents a potential future engagement. It is the Gallery's prospect board.

## What belongs here

- Job applications and cover letters (drafts and submitted records)
- Grant and fellowship proposals
- Collaboration inquiries and leads
- Conference and speaking opportunity entries
- Status tracking for each opportunity (applied, shortlisted, accepted, rejected)

## What does NOT belong here

- General correspondence not tied to an opportunity → [[../Correspondence/README.md|Correspondence]]
- Active project work → [[../../3. Workshop/Prototyping/README.md|Prototyping]]
- Personal financial records → [[../../5. Quarters/Finance/README.md|Finance]]
- Closed opportunities that are no longer actionable → [[../../7. Archive/Completed Projects/README.md|Completed Projects]] or [[../../7. Archive/Historical/README.md|Historical]]

## Tips

- **Status is mandatory.** Every opportunity note requires a status: `identified`, `preparing`, `submitted`, `shortlisted`, `accepted`, `rejected`, `withdrawn`. This drives pipeline tracking.
- **Deadline and follow-up.** Include the deadline and expected response window. Surface approaching deadlines and overdue follow-ups during review.
- **Tailor and track.** Keep records of what was submitted to whom and when. Avoid generic applications without customization notes.
- **Post-mortem valuable.** When an opportunity closes (especially rejected ones), briefly note what was learned. This informs future attempts.
