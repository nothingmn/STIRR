# Opportunities

Applications, proposals, and leads — the outward-facing pursuits that may become real work.

## What belongs here

- Job applications and cover letters (drafts and submitted records)
- Grant and fellowship proposals
- Collaboration inquiries and leads
- Conference and speaking opportunity entries
- Status tracking for each opportunity (applied, shortlisted, accepted, rejected)

## What does NOT belong here

- General correspondence not tied to an opportunity (that goes to [[../Correspondence/PROTOCOL|Correspondence Protocol]])
- Active project work (that goes to [[../../3. Workshop/Prototyping/PROTOCOL|Prototyping Protocol]])
- Personal financial records (those go to [[../../5. Quarters/Finance/PROTOCOL|Finance Protocol]])
- Closed opportunities that are no longer actionable (move to [[../../7. Archive/Historical/PROTOCOL|Historical Protocol]] or [[../../7. Archive/Obsolete/PROTOCOL|Obsolete Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Bridge mode.** The LLM may draft application materials, track deadlines, and format proposals. All submissions must be human-reviewed before sending.
2. **Deadline and follow-up.** Include the deadline and expected response window. The LLM should surface approaching deadlines and overdue follow-ups.
