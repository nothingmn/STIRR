# Correspondence

Drafts and sent communications — letters, emails, and messages composed for specific recipients.

## What belongs here

- Draft emails and letters (outgoing only)
- Records of sent communications
- Templates for recurring correspondence types
- Follow-up notes tied to specific communications
- Communication logs for ongoing exchanges

## What does NOT belong here

- Received correspondence (file in Quarters/People or Archive as appropriate)
- Personal reflections about correspondence (those go to [[../../5. Quarters/Living/PROTOCOL|Living Protocol]])
- Published articles or essays (those go to [[../../1. Study/Writing/PROTOCOL|Writing Protocol]])
- Opportunity and application drafts (those go to [[../Opportunities/PROTOCOL|Opportunities Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Bridge mode.** The LLM may draft, format, and proofread correspondence. All LLM-drafted text must be reviewed before sending. The human retains full authorial responsibility.
2. **Confidentiality.** Some correspondence is sensitive. Use the `llm_access` frontmatter property or tags to restrict LLM involvement on a per-file basis when needed.
3. **Close the loop.** If a sent communication requires a response, note it. The LLM may surface pending follow-ups during review.
