# Correspondence

Drafts and sent communications — letters, emails, and messages composed for specific recipients. This area holds outgoing correspondence: draft emails, letters, formal communications, and records of sent messages. It is the Gallery's writing desk, where outward-facing prose is composed and tracked.

## What belongs here

- Draft emails and letters (outgoing only)
- Records of sent communications
- Templates for recurring correspondence types
- Follow-up notes tied to specific communications
- Communication logs for ongoing exchanges

## What does NOT belong here

- Received correspondence → file in [[../../5. Quarters/People/README.md|People]] or [[../../7. Archive/README.md|Archive]] as appropriate
- Personal reflections about correspondence → [[../../5. Quarters/Living/README.md|Living]]
- Published articles or essays → [[../../1. Study/Writing/README.md|Writing]]
- Opportunity and application drafts → [[../Opportunities/README.md|Opportunities]]

## Tips

- **Recipient-first naming.** Name correspondence files by recipient and date: `YYYY-MM-DD RecipientName.md`. This makes chronological review straightforward.
- **Log sent items.** When a communication is sent, update its status to `sent` and add a sent date. Do not delete drafts — they serve as a record.
- **Confidentiality.** Some correspondence is sensitive. Use frontmatter tags to restrict processing on a per-file basis when needed.
- **Close the loop.** If a sent communication requires a response, note it. Surface pending follow-ups during review.
