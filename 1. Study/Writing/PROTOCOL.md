# Writing

Essays, articles, and drafts in progress — the place where thinking becomes prose.

## What belongs here

- Drafts of essays, articles, and papers
- Revision histories and editorial notes
- Published pieces (with a `status: published` property)
- Outlines and structural skeletons for planned works
- Abandoned drafts retained for salvage value

## What does NOT belong here

- Raw research notes (those belong in [[../Research/PROTOCOL|Research Protocol]])
- Half-formed ideas without narrative structure (those go to [[../Thought Streams/PROTOCOL|Thought Streams Protocol]])
- Correspondence and letters (those go to [[../../4. Gallery/Correspondence/PROTOCOL|Correspondence Protocol]])
- Source material and references (those go to [[../Sources/PROTOCOL|Sources Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Guard the voice.** Writing carries authorial voice. The LLM may critique, suggest alternatives, and copy-edit — but it does not generate substantive prose on behalf of the author.
