# Synthesis

Cross-domain connections and integrated insight — where separate threads are woven together.

## What belongs here

- Notes that bridge two or more research threads
- Conceptual frameworks and mental models
- "Connection" notes linking ideas from different domains
- Summaries that integrate findings from multiple sources
- Meta-analyses and pattern-level observations

## What does NOT belong here

- Single-domain research notes (those belong in [[../Research/PROTOCOL|Research Protocol]])
- Drafts of essays (those belong in [[../Writing/PROTOCOL|Writing Protocol]])
- Unprocessed raw ideas (those belong in [[../Thought Streams/PROTOCOL|Thought Streams Protocol]])
- Reference data (those belong in [[../../2. Library/General Reference/PROTOCOL|General Reference Protocol]] or [[../Sources/PROTOCOL|Sources Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Guard inference.** The LLM may propose connections and suggest patterns, but synthesis claims — especially ones that bridge domains — require human validation. The LLM flags; the human decides.
