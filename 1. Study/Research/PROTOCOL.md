# Research

Active research and deep investigations — the sustained inquiry that drives understanding forward.

## What belongs here

- Active investigation notes and working hypotheses
- Literature reviews and reading annotations
- Research questions and open problems
- Methodological notes and experimental observations
- Threads that are still being explored, not yet resolved

## What does NOT belong here

- Resolved research that has been written up (move to [[../Writing/PROTOCOL|Writing Protocol]] or [[../../2. Library/General Reference/PROTOCOL|General Reference Protocol]])
- Raw source material still needing annotation (that goes to [[../Sources/PROTOCOL|Sources Protocol]])
- Half-formed ideas not yet ready for structured inquiry (those go to [[../Thought Streams/PROTOCOL|Thought Streams Protocol]])
- Reference material on stable topics (that goes to [[../../2. Library/General Reference/PROTOCOL|General Reference Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Guard access.** Research notes are the product of sustained human thinking. The LLM may suggest, question, and connect — but it does not rewrite research conclusions or fill gaps by inference.
2. **Interlocutor, not author.** The LLM's role is to challenge, probe, and connect — not to produce research output on its own.
