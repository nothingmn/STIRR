# Research

Active research and deep investigations — the sustained inquiry that drives understanding forward. This area holds notes from active research: literature reviews, investigation threads, field notes, and working hypotheses. Research is the engine room of the Study; it is where questions are pursued before they become conclusions.

## What belongs here

- Active investigation notes and working hypotheses
- Literature reviews and reading annotations
- Research questions and open problems
- Methodological notes and experimental observations
- Threads that are still being explored, not yet resolved

## What does NOT belong here

- Resolved research that has been written up → [[../Writing/README.md|Writing]] or [[../Synthesis/README.md|Synthesis]]
- Raw source material still needing annotation → [[../Sources/README.md|Sources]]
- Half-formed ideas not yet ready for structured inquiry → [[../Thought Streams/README.md|Thought Streams]]
- Reference material on stable topics → [[../../2. Library/General Reference/README.md|General Reference]]

## Tips

- **Name by topic.** Use clear, descriptive titles that convey the research question or domain. Avoid vague names like "Notes" or "Research 3."
- **Link to sources.** Every research note should link to the sources it draws from. Provenance matters.
- **Mark status.** Use tags or properties (e.g., `status: active`, `status: dormant`) so that active threads are distinguishable from parked ones.
