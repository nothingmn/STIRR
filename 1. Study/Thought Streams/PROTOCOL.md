# Thought Streams

Emerging ideas and half-formed thinking — the pre-research space where insight first surfaces.

## What belongs here

- Early-stage ideas not yet ready for formal research
- Questions, hunches, and intuitions worth tracking
- Exploratory thinking and associative chains
- Notes from conversations that sparked new directions
- "Something about..." fragments that may grow into more

## What does NOT belong here

- Research with defined methodology and sources (move to [[../Research/PROTOCOL|Research Protocol]])
- Drafts with narrative shape (move to [[../Writing/PROTOCOL|Writing Protocol]])
- Processed and connected insights (move to [[../Synthesis/PROTOCOL|Synthesis Protocol]])
- Quick captures still needing triage (those stay in [[../../0. Gatehouse/Inbox/PROTOCOL|Inbox Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Protect the fragile.** Thought streams are the most vulnerable material in the vault. The LLM must not prematurely structure, judge, or "fix" these notes. Its role is to listen and occasionally ask a clarifying question.
