# Thought Streams

Emerging ideas and half-formed thinking — the pre-research space where insight first surfaces. This area is for ideas that have not yet hardened into structured research or writing: sparks, hunches, questions without answers, and threads of thinking still too raw for formal treatment. It is the seedbed of the Study.

## What belongs here

- Early-stage ideas not yet ready for formal research
- Questions, hunches, and intuitions worth tracking
- Exploratory thinking and associative chains
- Notes from conversations that sparked new directions
- "Something about..." fragments that may grow into more

## What does NOT belong here

- Research with defined methodology and sources → [[../Research/README.md|Research]]
- Drafts with narrative shape → [[../Writing/README.md|Writing]]
- Processed and connected insights → [[../Synthesis/README.md|Synthesis]]
- Quick captures still needing triage → [[../../0. Gatehouse/Inbox/README.md|Inbox]]

## Tips

- **Date and tag.** Add a date to every thought stream entry. Use loose tags so related fragments can be found later even if their titles differ.
- **No minimum quality.** A single sentence is valid here. Do not expand or justify a fragment to make it look more substantial.
- **Promote when ready.** When a thought stream matures into something structured, move it to Research or Writing and leave a link behind.
- **Let dead streams rest.** Not every fragment grows up. It is fine to leave dormant threads here — they serve as a record of paths considered.
