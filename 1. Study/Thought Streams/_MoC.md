# Thought Streams

Emerging ideas and half-formed thinking — the pre-research space where insight first surfaces. This area is for ideas that have not yet hardened into structured research or writing: sparks, hunches, questions without answers, and threads of thinking still too raw for formal treatment. It is the seedbed of the Study.
