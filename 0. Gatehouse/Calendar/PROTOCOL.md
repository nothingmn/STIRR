# Calendar

Time-bound items, deadlines, and events — anything anchored to a specific date or window.

## What belongs here

- Deadlines with specific due dates
- Scheduled meetings, appointments, and events
- Recurring reminders and seasonal tasks
- Planning views (weekly, monthly, quarterly outlooks)
- Time-sensitive opportunities with expiry dates

## What does NOT belong here

- Daily reflections or journal entries (those go to [[../Daily/PROTOCOL|Daily Protocol]])
- Unsorted quick captures (those go to [[../Inbox/PROTOCOL|Inbox Protocol]])
- Project tasks without hard deadlines (those belong in Workshop or the relevant project room)
- Historical records of past events (those move to [[../../7. Archive/Historical/PROTOCOL|Historical Protocol]] once closed)

## Area-specific rules

1. **Every item needs a date.** If there is no time anchor, the item does not belong here.
2. **LLM as scribe.** The assistant may log events, format calendar entries, and surface upcoming deadlines — but it does not make scheduling decisions without confirmation.
