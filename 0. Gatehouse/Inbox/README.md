# Inbox

Uncaptured material, quick notes, and anything that has not yet been sorted into its proper place. The Inbox is the staging area for unprocessed input — ideas jotted in haste, links saved without context, fragments that need triage. Nothing lives here permanently; everything either moves on or is discarded.

## What belongs here

- Quick-capture notes with no clear home yet
- Links, screenshots, or clippings saved for later consideration
- Fragments and voice memos awaiting transcription or context
- Material pulled from external sources before triage

## What does NOT belong here

- Notes that have already been triaged and filed — move them to their proper room
- Daily journal entries → [[../Daily/README.md|Daily]]
- Calendar items with specific dates → [[../Calendar/README.md|Calendar]]
- Anything you intend to keep long-term — the Inbox is temporary holding, not storage

## Tips

- **Zero on a cycle.** Empty the Inbox on a regular cadence (daily or weekly). Every item gets filed, acted on, or deleted.
- **No polishing.** Do not refine inbox items in place. Process them into their destination, possibly rewriting as you go.
- **Timestamp captures.** When possible, note when material was captured so triage can prioritize by recency.
- **Naming is loose.** Inbox files need not follow strict naming conventions — but make them descriptive enough that triage is straightforward.
