# Inbox

Uncaptured material, quick notes, and anything that has not yet been sorted into its proper place.

## What belongs here

- Quick-capture notes with no clear home yet
- Links, screenshots, or clippings saved for later consideration
- Fragments and voice memos awaiting transcription or context
- Material pulled from external sources before triage

## What does NOT belong here

- Notes that have already been triaged and filed — move them to their proper room
- Daily journal entries (those go to [[../Daily/PROTOCOL|Daily Protocol]])
- Calendar items with specific dates (those go to [[../Calendar/PROTOCOL|Calendar Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **LLM as scribe.** The assistant may help transcribe or reformat inbox captures but does not decide where items belong without human direction.
