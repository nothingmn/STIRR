# Daily

Daily notes and journal entries captured in the moment — the running record of each day.

## What belongs here

- Daily notes keyed to a date
- Morning pages, evening reviews, stream-of-consciousness captures
- End-of-day summaries and highlights
- Notes tagged or linked to specific calendar dates

## What does NOT belong here

- Material that has been processed and filed into its proper room — move it out once it has a home
- Recurring templates or structural notes (those go to [[../../8. Foundation/Templates/PROTOCOL|Templates Protocol]])
- Time-bound deadlines and events (those go to [[../Calendar/PROTOCOL|Calendar Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **LLM as scribe.** The assistant may transcribe, format, and lightly organize — but it does not generate daily content or fill in blanks from inference.
