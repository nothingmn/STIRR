# Daily

Daily notes and journal entries captured in the moment — the running record of each day. This area holds day-by-day notes: reflections, event logs, and whatever surfaces during the course of a single day. It is the first place the day gets recorded and the last place it should be rewritten.

## What belongs here

- Daily notes keyed to a date (YYYY-MM-DD or similar convention)
- Morning pages, evening reviews, stream-of-consciousness captures
- End-of-day summaries and highlights
- Notes tagged or linked to specific calendar dates

## What does NOT belong here

- Material that has been processed and filed into its proper room — move it out once it has a home
- Recurring templates or structural notes → [[../../8. Foundation/Templates/README.md|Templates]]
- Time-bound deadlines and events → [[../Calendar/README.md|Calendar]]

## Tips

- **One note per day.** Use a consistent naming convention like `YYYY-MM-DD.md`. Do not fragment a single day across multiple files.
- **Captured, not curated.** Daily notes are raw capture. Resist the urge to polish. Refinement happens downstream.
- **Link outward.** When a daily note touches on a project, person, or topic that has its own note elsewhere, link to it. The daily note is a hub, not a silo.
- **Archive naturally.** Past daily notes remain here. They do not need to be moved to Archive unless they contain project-specific material worth preserving separately.
