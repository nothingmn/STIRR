# Health

Medical records, sleep data, and biometric information — the body's documentation. This area holds health-related notes: medical records, sleep logs, biometric data, fitness tracking, and anything related to physical and mental well-being. Health data is among the most sensitive material in the vault.

## What belongs here

- Medical records and appointment notes
- Sleep logs and biometric data exports
- Medication and supplement tracking
- Fitness and exercise logs
- Mental health notes and therapy records
- Symptom tracking and health timelines

## What does NOT belong here

- Personal financial records → [[../Finance/README.md|Finance]]
- Recreational activity logs unless tied to health outcomes → [[../Recreation/README.md|Recreation]]
- General daily notes about how you feel → [[../Living/README.md|Living]]
- People notes about healthcare providers → [[../People/README.md|People]]

## Tips

- **Encrypt or isolate.** Where possible, use Obsidian encryption plugins or store particularly sensitive records outside the synced vault. Health data warrants extra protection.
- **Date everything.** Every health entry needs a date. Medical timelines depend on accurate chronology.
- **Structure for retrieval.** Use consistent headings and tags so that historical health data can be searched when needed (e.g., during medical appointments).
- **Do not self-diagnose here.** Health notes record observations and professional guidance. They are not a substitute for medical advice.
