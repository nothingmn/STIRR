# Recreation

Hobbies, sports, outdoors, and travel — the restorative dimensions of life.

## What belongs here

- Hobby notes, project logs, and technique records
- Sports and fitness activity logs (for enjoyment, not medical tracking)
- Travel plans, itineraries, and trip reports
- Reading, watching, and listening logs (recreational media)
- Equipment and gear notes for recreational pursuits

## What does NOT belong here

- Health-driven fitness tracking (that goes to [[../Health/PROTOCOL|Health Protocol]])
- Financial planning for travel or hobbies (that goes to [[../Finance/PROTOCOL|Finance Protocol]])
- Professional travel (that goes to [[../../4. Gallery/Opportunities/PROTOCOL|Opportunities Protocol]] or project notes)
- General daily notes that happen to mention recreation (those go to [[../Living/PROTOCOL|Living Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Restricted LLM access.** The LLM does not read, generate, or modify recreation notes. This area is human-only. The `llm_role: none` setting is intentional.
