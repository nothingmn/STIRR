# Recreation

Hobbies, sports, outdoors, and travel — the restorative dimensions of life. This area holds notes about recreational activities: hobbies, sports, outdoor adventures, travel plans, and anything done for enjoyment. Recreation is personal and restorative; it is documented to enrich, not to optimize.

## What belongs here

- Hobby notes, project logs, and technique records
- Sports and fitness activity logs (for enjoyment, not medical tracking)
- Travel plans, itineraries, and trip reports
- Reading, watching, and listening logs (recreational media)
- Equipment and gear notes for recreational pursuits

## What does NOT belong here

- Health-driven fitness tracking → [[../Health/README.md|Health]]
- Financial planning for travel or hobbies → [[../Finance/README.md|Finance]]
- Professional travel → [[../../4. Gallery/Opportunities/README.md|Opportunities]] or project notes
- General daily notes that happen to mention recreation → [[../../0. Gatehouse/Daily/README.md|Daily]]

## Tips

- **Enjoyment first.** Do not turn recreation into work. Logs and notes here serve memory and reflection, not productivity metrics.
- **Loose structure welcome.** Recreation notes need not follow strict templates. A travel journal can be a narrative; a hobby log can be a simple list.
- **Link to people.** When recreation involves others, link to the relevant [[../People/README.md|People]] notes rather than duplicating names and details.
- **Seasonal review.** Recreation preferences change. Review this area annually and retire notes for activities no longer pursued.
