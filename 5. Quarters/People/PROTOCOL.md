# People

Relationships, family, and contacts — the personal network in all its dimensions.

## What belongs here

- Contact notes for individuals (family, friends, colleagues)
- Relationship context and history
- Gift logs, birthday reminders, and personal milestones
- Notes from conversations and meetings with specific people
- Group or community notes where the people dimension is primary

## What does NOT belong here

- Professional correspondence (that goes to [[../../4. Gallery/Correspondence/PROTOCOL|Correspondence Protocol]])
- Professional opportunities (those go to [[../../4. Gallery/Opportunities/PROTOCOL|Opportunities Protocol]])
- Financial records tied to people (those go to [[../Finance/PROTOCOL|Finance Protocol]])
- Health data about individuals (that goes to [[../Health/PROTOCOL|Health Protocol]])
- General personal notes not tied to a person (those go to [[../Living/PROTOCOL|Living Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Restricted LLM access.** The LLM does not read, generate, or modify people notes. This area is human-only. The `llm_role: none` setting is intentional and non-negotiable.
