# People

Relationships, family, and contacts — the personal network in all its dimensions. This area holds notes about people: family, friends, colleagues, and contacts. It tracks relationships, contact details, and the social fabric that connects daily life to the wider world.

## What belongs here

- Contact notes for individuals (family, friends, colleagues)
- Relationship context and history
- Gift logs, birthday reminders, and personal milestones
- Notes from conversations and meetings with specific people
- Group or community notes where the people dimension is primary

## What does NOT belong here

- Professional correspondence → [[../../4. Gallery/Correspondence/README.md|Correspondence]]
- Professional opportunities → [[../../4. Gallery/Opportunities/README.md|Opportunities]]
- Financial records tied to people → [[../Finance/README.md|Finance]]
- Health data about individuals → [[../Health/README.md|Health]]
- General personal notes not tied to a person → [[../Living/README.md|Living]]

## Tips

- **One person, one note.** Each individual gets a single note. Add to it over time rather than creating fragments.
- **Respect privacy.** Do not store sensitive personal information (passwords, financial details, medical data) in people notes. Those belong in their proper restricted areas.
- **Date interactions.** When logging a conversation or meeting, include the date. This creates a natural timeline within each person's note.
- **Discretion in naming.** Use initials or first names where full identification is unnecessary. The vault author knows who "M." is; the filesystem does not need to.
