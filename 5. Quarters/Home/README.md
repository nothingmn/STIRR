# Home

Property, appliances, and household records — the physical infrastructure of daily life. This area holds notes about the home: property records, appliance manuals, maintenance logs, renovation plans, and anything related to the physical space and its contents. It is the household inventory and maintenance register.

## What belongs here

- Property records and lease/mortgage documents
- Appliance and equipment manuals, warranties, and serial numbers
- Home maintenance and repair logs
- Renovation and improvement plans
- Utility records and service provider contacts
- Household inventory lists

## What does NOT belong here

- Financial records tied to the home (mortgage statements) → [[../Finance/README.md|Finance]]
- Legal documents (contracts) → [[../../6. Counting House/Legal/README.md|Legal]]
- Health and safety notes not tied to physical infrastructure → [[../Health/README.md|Health]]
- General daily notes about home life → [[../Living/README.md|Living]]

## Tips

- **Serial numbers and warranties.** Record these when you acquire an item. They are impossible to reconstruct later.
- **Maintenance log.** Keep a running log of repairs and maintenance with dates. This builds a service history that supports future decisions and warranty claims.
- **Link to finance.** When a home note involves significant cost, link to the corresponding Finance note. The two areas cross-reference naturally.
- **Photo references.** Where possible, note the location of related photos. Keep media outside the vault if it is large.
