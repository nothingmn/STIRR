# Home

Property, appliances, and household records — the physical infrastructure of daily life.

## What belongs here

- Property records and lease/mortgage documents
- Appliance and equipment manuals, warranties, and serial numbers
- Home maintenance and repair logs
- Renovation and improvement plans
- Utility records and service provider contacts
- Household inventory lists

## What does NOT belong here

- Financial records tied to the home (mortgage statements go to [[../Finance/PROTOCOL|Finance Protocol]])
- Legal documents (contracts go to [[../../6. Counting House/Legal/PROTOCOL|Legal Protocol]])
- Health and safety notes not tied to physical infrastructure (those go to [[../Health/PROTOCOL|Health Protocol]])
- General daily notes about home life (those go to [[../Living/PROTOCOL|Living Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Restricted LLM access.** The LLM does not read, generate, or modify home notes. This area is human-only. The `llm_role: none` setting is intentional.
