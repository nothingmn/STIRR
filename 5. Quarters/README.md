# The Quarters

Everything personal but not primarily intellectual: health, family, finances, hobbies, the material conditions of life. The sanctuary of the palace. This is where the LLM restrictions are the tightest in the entire vault -- personal, tacit, emotionally embedded knowledge is the most vulnerable to analytic displacement, and the Quarters protect it.

## How this room works

MIXED mode, human-primary substrate, LLM restricted. The LLM has no active role here. It may format, calculate, or search on explicit request only. It may not initiate work, offer interpretations, suggest optimizations, or generate content about your relationships, health, or personal experience. The LLM does not enter uninvited.

## What you do here

- **This is your personal space.** The LLM is restricted here for a reason.
- **Personal, tacit, emotionally embedded knowledge is the most vulnerable to displacement.** The Conjugate Pair trade-off is most damaging here.
- **You may ask the LLM to help with mechanical tasks** (formatting, calculating, searching). That is all.
- **If you need to think through a personal matter, do it yourself first.** Attempt before documentation applies here too.
- **Business finances go to the Counting House, not here.** Personal financial planning (Quarters/Finance) vs. business financial records (Counting House).

## What moves out

- Business financial records that ended up here should migrate to the Counting House
- Personal matters that develop into intellectual projects may migrate to the Study or Gallery
