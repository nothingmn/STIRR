# Living

Day-to-day personal notes that do not fit a more specific category — the fabric of ordinary life. This area holds personal notes about daily living: routines, preferences, plans, and observations that are too general for other Quarters areas but too personal for the Gatehouse. It is the catch-all for the personal.

## What belongs here

- Routine and habit notes
- Personal preferences and reference lists (e.g., "my coffee order")
- Life plans and personal goals
- Notes about household rhythms and logistics
- Personal reflections not tied to a specific day

## What does NOT belong here

- Daily journal entries → [[../../0. Gatehouse/Daily/README.md|Daily]]
- Health data → [[../Health/README.md|Health]]
- Financial records → [[../Finance/README.md|Finance]]
- People-specific notes → [[../People/README.md|People]]
- Hobby and recreation notes → [[../Recreation/README.md|Recreation]]

## Tips

- **Personal, not professional.** If a note is primarily about work, it does not belong here. Route it to the appropriate professional room.
- **Allow imperfection.** Living notes are allowed to be messy, partial, and subjective. They do not need to serve any audience beyond the author.
- **Link to people.** When daily life involves others, link to People notes. Do not duplicate personal details about others here.
- **Review when it matters.** Unlike some areas, Living notes do not need a strict review cycle. Clean up when it feels right, not on a schedule.
