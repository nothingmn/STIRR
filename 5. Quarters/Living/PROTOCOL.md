# Living

Day-to-day personal notes that do not fit a more specific category — the fabric of ordinary life.

## What belongs here

- Routine and habit notes
- Personal preferences and reference lists (e.g., "my coffee order")
- Life plans and personal goals
- Notes about household rhythms and logistics
- Personal reflections not tied to a specific day

## What does NOT belong here

- Daily journal entries (those go to [[../../0. Gatehouse/Daily/PROTOCOL|Daily Protocol]])
- Health data (that goes to [[../Health/PROTOCOL|Health Protocol]])
- Financial records (those go to [[../Finance/PROTOCOL|Finance Protocol]])
- People-specific notes (those go to [[../People/PROTOCOL|People Protocol]])
- Hobby and recreation notes (those go to [[../Recreation/PROTOCOL|Recreation Protocol]])

## Area-specific rules

1. **Restricted LLM access.** The LLM does not read, generate, or modify living notes. This area is human-only. The `llm_role: none` setting is intentional and non-negotiable.
