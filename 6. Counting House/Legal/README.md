# Legal

Contracts, employment documents, and legal matters — the binding obligations and protections. This area holds legal documents and records: contracts, employment agreements, intellectual property filings, dispute records, and any material with legal significance. Legal records are authoritative; they must be accurate, complete, and carefully managed.

## What belongs here

- Contracts and agreements (signed and drafts)
- Employment documents (offer letters, NDAs, non-competes)
- Intellectual property records (trademarks, copyrights, patents)
- Dispute and litigation records
- Legal correspondence and attorney communications

## What does NOT belong here

- Tax filings → [[../Tax/README.md|Tax]]
- Regulatory compliance filings → [[../Compliance/README.md|Compliance]]
- General correspondence → [[../../4. Gallery/Correspondence/README.md|Correspondence]]
- Personal financial records → [[../../5. Quarters/Finance/README.md|Finance]]
- Property records → [[../../5. Quarters/Home/README.md|Home]]

## Tips

- **Do not modify signed documents.** Once a contract is signed, the file is immutable. Amendments and addenda are separate documents.
- **Version control for drafts.** Track contract draft versions explicitly: `v1`, `v2`, `final`, `signed`. Do not overwrite an earlier draft with a later one.
- **Expiration tracking.** Note contract start and end dates. Surface upcoming expirations, renewals, and termination windows during review.
- **Confidentiality flags.** Some legal documents are subject to NDA or privilege. Use frontmatter tags to flag confidentiality level and restrict processing accordingly.
- **Professional review.** Legal notes supplement professional legal advice; they do not replace it. Note when a document has been reviewed by counsel.
