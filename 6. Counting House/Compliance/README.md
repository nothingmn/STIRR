# Compliance

Regulatory filings, audit records, and compliance documentation — the evidentiary chain for regulated obligations. This area holds compliance-related records: regulatory filings, audit reports, certifications, and documentation required by governing bodies. Compliance records must be complete, traceable, and retained for mandated periods.

## What belongs here

- Regulatory filings and their supporting documentation
- Audit reports and audit trails
- Certifications and license records
- Compliance checklists and attestations
- Policy acknowledgments and training records

## What does NOT belong here

- Tax filings → [[../Tax/README.md|Tax]]
- Contracts → [[../Legal/README.md|Legal]]
- General financial records → [[../Accounts/README.md|Accounts]]
- Employment agreements → [[../Legal/README.md|Legal]]
- Personal records → the appropriate [[../../5. Quarters/README.md|Quarters]] area

## Tips

- **Regulator and year structure.** Organize by regulatory body and filing period: `RegulatorName/YYYY/`. This mirrors how regulators expect records to be produced.
- **Retention mandates.** Compliance records must be retained for the legally required period. Do not delete, archive, or obscure any compliance filing.
- **Audit-ready.** Every compliance record should be stored as if an auditor will request it tomorrow.
- **Version integrity.** Once filed, compliance documents must not be modified. Corrections are filed as amendments.
- **Expiration and renewal.** Track license and certification expiration dates. Surface upcoming renewals well before deadlines.
