# Compliance

Regulatory filings, audit records, and compliance documentation — the evidentiary chain for regulated obligations.

## What belongs here

- Regulatory filings and their supporting documentation
- Audit reports and audit trails
- Certifications and license records
- Compliance checklists and attestations
- Policy acknowledgments and training records

## What does NOT belong here

- Tax filings (those go to [[../Tax/PROTOCOL|Tax Protocol]])
- Contracts (those go to [[../Legal/PROTOCOL|Legal Protocol]])
- General financial records (those go to [[../Accounts/PROTOCOL|Accounts Protocol]])
- Employment agreements (those go to [[../Legal/PROTOCOL|Legal Protocol]])
- Personal records (those go to the appropriate Quarters area)

## Area-specific rules

1. **Retention mandates.** Compliance records must be retained for the legally required period. Do not delete, archive, or obscure any compliance filing.
2. **Version integrity.** Once filed, compliance documents must not be modified. Corrections are filed as amendments. The LLM must not alter filed records.
3. **Expiration and renewal.** Track license and certification expiration dates. The LLM should surface upcoming renewals well before deadlines.
